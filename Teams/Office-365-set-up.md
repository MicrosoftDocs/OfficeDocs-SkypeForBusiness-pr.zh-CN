<a name="set-up-microsoft-teams-in-your-office-365-organization"></a><span data-ttu-id="ccf3b-103">在你的 Office 365 组织中设置 Microsoft Teams</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="ccf3b-103">Set up Microsoft Teams in your Office 365 organization</span></span>
======================================================

<span data-ttu-id="ccf3b-104">默认情况下，对于在使用预览版期间未尝试使用 Microsoft Teams 的组织启用 Microsoft Teams。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="ccf3b-104">By default, Microsoft Teams is enabled for organizations who have not tried Microsoft Teams during the preview.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="ccf3b-105">对于已在预览版中测试过的组织，将保留在正式发布 Microsoft Teams 之前所做的设置。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="ccf3b-105">For organizations who have been testing in preview, the setting will remain as what was set prior to general availability of Microsoft Teams.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="ccf3b-106">作为贵组织的管理员，你可以为用户分配许可证以控制对 Microsoft Teams 的个人访问，并且你可以允许或阻止可以在 Microsoft Teams 中使用的内容源。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="ccf3b-106">As an administrator for your organization, you can assign user licenses to control individual access to Microsoft Teams and you can allow or block what content sources can be used in Microsoft Teams.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="ccf3b-107">有关更多详细信息，请参阅 [Microsoft Teams 的管理员设置](https://support.office.com/article/3966a3f5-7e0f-4ea9-a402-41888f455ba2)。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="ccf3b-107">See [Administrator settings for Microsoft Teams](https://support.office.com/article/3966a3f5-7e0f-4ea9-a402-41888f455ba2) for more details.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="ccf3b-108">希望更改是否启用 Microsoft Teams 的组织可以按照下面的步骤在其 Office 365 租户中操作：</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="ccf3b-108">Organizations who wish to change whether Microsoft Teams is enabled or not can follow the steps below in their Office 365 tenant:</span></span>

1.  <span data-ttu-id="ccf3b-109">使用具有全局管理员权限的帐户登录 [Office 365 管理中心](https://go.microsoft.com/fwlink/?linkid=854665)。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="ccf3b-109">Sign in to [Office 365 Admin Center](https://go.microsoft.com/fwlink/?linkid=854665) with an account that has Global Administrator privileges.</span></span>

2.  <span data-ttu-id="ccf3b-110">导航到“**设置**” > “**服务和外接程序**”。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="ccf3b-110">Navigate to **Settings** > **Services & add-ins**.</span></span>

    ![<span data-ttu-id="ccf3b-111">Office 365 管理中心中的“设置”部分（选择了“服务和外接程序”）屏幕截图。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="ccf3b-111">Screenshot of the Settings section in the Office 365 admin center with Services & add-ins selected.</span></span> ](media/Set_up_Microsoft_Teams_in_your_Office_365_organization_image1.png)

3.  <span data-ttu-id="ccf3b-112">在“服务和外接程序”页面上，单击**“Microsoft Teams”**。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="ccf3b-112">On the Services & add-ins page, click **Microsoft Teams**.</span></span>

    ![选择了“Microsoft Teams”的“服务和外接程序”页面屏幕截图。](media/Set_up_Microsoft_Teams_in_your_Office_365_organization_image2.png)

    ![Microsoft Teams 设置页面屏幕截图，显示切换设置为“开启”以启用 Microsoft Teams。](media/Set_up_Microsoft_Teams_in_your_Office_365_organization_image3.png)
