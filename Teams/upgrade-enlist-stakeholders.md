> [!Tip]
> <span data-ttu-id="f3899-109">请花些时间确定理想的执行发起人、可以帮助设定项目色调、按示例引导并让其他人负责的人。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="f3899-109">Take time to identify the ideal executive sponsor, someone who can help set the tone of your project, leads by example, and holds others accountable.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="f3899-110">这应该是对项目范围和愿景具有权限的高层经理或高层。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="f3899-110">This should be a senior manager or executive who has authority over the project scope and vision.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="f3899-111">他们还应该是消除障碍和解决冲突的最终决策者，以帮助保持项目在预算上及时进行。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="f3899-111">They should also be the final decision-maker in removing blockers and resolving conflict to help keep the project on time and on budget.</span></span>
