<span data-ttu-id="9c7a0-105">本文是你的升级过程的项目利益干系人阶段的一部分，你为项目成功完成的第一个和最基本的活动。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="9c7a0-105">This article is part of the Project Stakeholders stage of your upgrade journey, the first and most essential activity you complete for your project's success.</span></span>
