    <span data-ttu-id="9b71d-235">延迟被按单向或往返时间。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="9b71d-235">Latency is measured as one-way or round-trip time.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="9b71d-241">[媒体质量和网络连接性能](https://docs.microsoft.com/SkypeForBusiness/optimizing-your-network/media-quality-and-network-connectivity-performance)介绍这些障碍的最大值。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="9b71d-241">The maximum values for these impairments are described in [Media quality and network connectivity performance](https://docs.microsoft.com/SkypeForBusiness/optimizing-your-network/media-quality-and-network-connectivity-performance).</span></span>

-   <span data-ttu-id="9b71d-247">*客户端段*是客户端驻留在其中的逻辑网络段。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="9b71d-247">The *client segment* is the logical network segment in which your clients reside.</span></span>
