<a name="overview-of-teams-and-channels-in-microsoft-teams"></a><span data-ttu-id="9edf4-103">Microsoft Teams 中的团队和频道概述</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="9edf4-103">Overview of teams and channels in Microsoft Teams</span></span>
=================================================

<span data-ttu-id="9edf4-104">我们首先来了解 Microsoft Teams 如何允许单个团队进行自我组织：</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="9edf4-104">Let’s get started by thinking about how Microsoft Teams allows individual teams to self-organize:</span></span>

-   <span data-ttu-id="9edf4-105">**团队**是与组织内不同项目和任务相关的人员、内容和工具的集合。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="9edf4-105">**Teams** are a collection of people, content, and tools surrounding different projects and jobs within an organization.</span></span>

    -   <span data-ttu-id="9edf4-106">可以创建只允许受邀用户加入的专用团队。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="9edf4-106">Teams can be created to be private to only invited users.</span></span>

    -   <span data-ttu-id="9edf4-107">也可以创建公用的开放团队，组织中的任何人都可以加入（最多 2500 个成员）。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="9edf4-107">Teams can also be created to be public and open and anyone within the organization can join (up to 2500 members).</span></span>

-   <span data-ttu-id="9edf4-108">**频道**是团队中的专用区域，用于保留按特定主题、项目、专业（适用于你的团队的任何内容！）组织的对话。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="9edf4-108">**Channels** are dedicated sections within a team to keep conversations organized by specific topics, projects, disciplines—whatever works for your team!</span></span>

    -   <span data-ttu-id="9edf4-109">团队频道是团队中的所有人都可以公开进行对话的地方。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="9edf4-109">Team channels are places where everyone on the team can openly have conversations.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="9edf4-110">私人聊天只有聊天中的人员可以看到。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="9edf4-110">Private chats are only visible to those people in the chat.</span></span>

    -   <span data-ttu-id="9edf4-111">可以使用包括选项卡、连接器和聊天机器人在内的应用扩展频道。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="9edf4-111">Channels can be extended with Apps that include Tabs, Connectors, and Bots.</span></span>

![Microsoft Teams 中团队和频道组织方式层次结构示意图。](media/Overview_of_teams_and_channels_in_Microsoft_Teams_image1.png)

<span data-ttu-id="9edf4-113">吸引用户使用 Microsoft Teams 的一项关键的早期计划活动是帮助人们考虑和了解 Microsoft Teams 如何在其日常生活中增强协作。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="9edf4-113">One key early planning activity to engage users with Microsoft Teams, is to help people think and understand how Microsoft Teams can enhance collaboration in their day to day lives.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="9edf4-114">默认情况下，所有用户都有权在 Microsoft Teams 中创建团队（要对此进行修改，请参阅[在 Teams 中指定角色和权限](assign-roles-permissions.md)）。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="9edf4-114">By default, all users have permissions to create a team within Microsoft Teams (to modify this, see here).</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="9edf4-115">现有 Office 365 组的用户也可以使用 Microsoft Teams 功能帮助自己。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="9edf4-115">Users of an existing Office 365 Group can also enhance them with Microsoft Teams functionality.</span></span>

<a name="sample-team-set-up"></a><span data-ttu-id="9edf4-116">团队建立示例</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="9edf4-116">Sample Team Set up</span></span>
------------------

<span data-ttu-id="9edf4-117">下面是一些操作示例，介绍了不同类型的用户如何建立其团队、频道和应用（选项卡/连接器/聊天机器人）- 要与你的用户社区开始有关 Microsoft Teams 的对话，这可能很有帮助作用。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="9edf4-117">Below are a few functional examples of how different types of users may approach setting up their teams, channels and apps (tabs/connectors/bots) – this may be useful to help kick off a conversation about Microsoft Teams with your user community.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="9edf4-118">当你考虑如何在贵组织中实施 Microsoft Teams 时，请注意，你可以指导如何建立团队，但用户可以控制如何自我组织。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="9edf4-118">As you think about how to implement Microsoft Teams in your organization, remember that you can provide guidance on how to structure their teams, however users have control of how they can self-organize.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="9edf4-119">这些示例正是促使团队开始仔细思考各种可能性。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="9edf4-119">These are just examples to help get teams to start thinking through the possibilities.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="9edf4-120">Microsoft Teams 的特点在于打破组织界限及推动跨职能团队，因此，鼓励你的用户从职能团队而不是组织界限角度考虑这一点。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="9edf4-120">Microsoft Teams is great at breaking down organizational silos and promoting cross-functional teams, so encourage your users to think about this as functional teams and not organizational silos.</span></span>
