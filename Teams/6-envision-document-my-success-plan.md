<span data-ttu-id="3ebb3-110">与转换组织中人员工作方式的任何技术实现一样，选择执行部署的正确方式将大大影响云语音实现的成功。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="3ebb3-110">As with any technology implementation that transforms the way people work in your organization, choosing the right way to undertake the deployment will greatly influence the success of your cloud voice implementation.</span></span>
