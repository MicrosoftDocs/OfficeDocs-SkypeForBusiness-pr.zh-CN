## <a name="deployment-model"></a><span data-ttu-id="dedb9-109">部署模型</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="dedb9-109">Deployment model</span></span> 

<span data-ttu-id="dedb9-110">与改变组织人员工作方式的任何技术实现一样，选择正确的部署方式将大大影响云语音实现的成功。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="dedb9-110">As with any technology implementation that transforms the way people work in your organization, choosing the right way to undertake the deployment will greatly influence the success of your cloud voice implementation.</span></span>
