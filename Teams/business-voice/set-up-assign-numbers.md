<span data-ttu-id="3fb32-104">在用户可以使用 Teams拨打或接听常规电话线路的电话呼叫之前，你需要为其分配电话号码。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="3fb32-104">Before users can use Teams to make or receive phone calls to or from regular phone lines, you need to assign phone numbers to them.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="3fb32-105">在Microsoft Teams客户端中，当用户单击"呼叫"时，分配给用户的电话号码将列在拨号 **盘中**。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="3fb32-105">In Microsoft Teams clients, the phone number that you assign to a user is listed in the dial pad when the user clicks **Calls**.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="3fb32-106">为需要电话号码的每个用户执行以下操作。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="3fb32-106">Do the following for each user that needs a phone number.</span></span>

> <span data-ttu-id="3fb32-108">如果看不到任何电话号码，请稍候。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="3fb32-108">If you don't see any phone numbers, please wait.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="3fb32-109">新电话号码可能需要几个小时才能在 Teams。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="3fb32-109">It may take several hours for your new phone numbers to become available in Teams.</span></span>

1. <span data-ttu-id="3fb32-111">在左侧导航窗格中，转到"<a href="https://admin.teams.microsoft.com/phone-numbers" target="_blank">**语音**  >  **"电话数字"。**</a></span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="3fb32-111">In the left navigation pane, go to <a href="https://admin.teams.microsoft.com/phone-numbers" target="_blank">**Voice** > **Phone numbers**</a>.</span></span>
1. <span data-ttu-id="3fb32-112">在 **"电话"** 页面上，选择列表中的未分配号码，然后单击"编辑 **"。**</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="3fb32-112">On the **Phone numbers** page, select an unassigned number in the list, and then click **Edit**.</span></span>
