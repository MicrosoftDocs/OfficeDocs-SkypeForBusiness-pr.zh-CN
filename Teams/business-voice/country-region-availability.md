<span data-ttu-id="6091e-103">Microsoft 365 商务语音在 70 多国家和地区可用。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="6091e-103">Microsoft 365 Business Voice is available in over 70 countries and regions.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="6091e-104">本文后面的表格中列出了商务语音可用的所有国家和地区。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="6091e-104">All of the countries and regions in which Business Voice is available are listed in the table later in this article.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="6091e-105">某些商务语音功能（特别是通话套餐和“入门”向导）仅在某些国家或地区可用。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="6091e-105">Some Business Voice features, specifically Calling Plans and the Getting Started Wizard, are available only in certain countries and regions.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="6091e-106">这些功能在租户所在位置是否可用确定了你是否可以自行设置商务语音，或者是否需要 Microsoft 合作伙伴或经销商的帮助。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="6091e-106">Whether these features are available in your tenant's location determines whether you can set up Business Voice on your own, or if you need help from a Microsoft partner or reseller.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="6091e-107">在**支持**通话套餐和“入门”向导的国家/地区，可从 Microsoft 购买商务语音，然后将现有电话号码设置或转移到 Microsoft。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="6091e-107">In countries and regions **with** support for Calling Plans and the Getting Started Wizard, you can buy Business Voice from Microsoft and then set up or transfer your existing phone numbers to Microsoft.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="6091e-108">执行此操作后，可在 Microsoft 365 中管理你的所有电话号码。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="6091e-108">Doing this will let you manage all of your phone numbers in Microsoft 365.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="6091e-109">完成向商务语音的迁移后：</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="6091e-109">After your move to Business Voice is finished:</span></span>

- <span data-ttu-id="6091e-110">Microsoft 365 将成为你的电话提供商，国内本地和长途电话均包含在商务语音中。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="6091e-110">Microsoft 365 becomes your telephone provider and domestic local and long distance calls are included in Business Voice.</span></span>  <span data-ttu-id="6091e-111">免费和国际长途电话费用直接由 Microsoft 收取。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="6091e-111">Toll free and international long distance charges are billed directly by Microsoft.</span></span>
- <span data-ttu-id="6091e-112">无需再订阅第三方电话提供商即可管理电话号码。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="6091e-112">You no longer need to subscribe to a third-party telephone provider to manage your phone numbers.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="6091e-114">如果你的租户所在的国家或地区支持通话套餐和“入门”向导，请查看[需要购买哪些产品才能使用有通话套餐的 Microsoft 365 商务语音](what-to-buy.md)。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="6091e-114">If your tenant is located in a country or region that supports Calling Plans and the Getting Started Wizard, check out [What do I need to buy to use Microsoft 365 Business Voice with Calling Plan?](what-to-buy.md).</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="6091e-115">在**不支持**通话套餐和“入门”向导的国家/地区，需要从 Microsoft 合作伙伴或经销商处购买商务语音。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="6091e-115">In countries and regions **without** support for Calling Plans and the Getting Started Wizard, you need to purchase Business Voice from a Microsoft partner or reseller.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="6091e-116">购买商务语音后，你需要与合作伙伴或经销商和第三方电话提供商一起设置本地电话服务硬件和 Microsoft 365 之间的直接路由。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="6091e-116">After you've purchased Business Voice, you need to then work with your partner or reseller and your third-party telephone provider to set up Direct Routing between your on-premises telephony hardware and Microsoft 365.</span></span>

- <span data-ttu-id="6091e-117">电话服务提供商不会更改。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="6091e-117">Your telephone provider doesn't change.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="6091e-118">所有本地、长途和免费费用均由现有的第三方电话提供商收费。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="6091e-118">All local, long distance, and toll-free charges are billed by your existing third-party telephone provider.</span></span>

- <span data-ttu-id="6091e-121">需要保留某些本地电话设备才能使本地电话设备（如桌面电话）与 Microsoft 365 进行通信。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="6091e-121">You need to maintain some on-premises telephone equipment to enable your on-premises telephony equipment (such as desk phones) to communicate with Microsoft 365.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="6091e-122">如果你的租户所在的国家或地区不支持通话套餐和“入门”向导，请查看[从 Microsoft 经销商或合作伙伴处获取帮助](reseller-partner-support.md)。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="6091e-122">If your tenant is located in a country or region that doesn't support Calling Plans and the Getting Started Wizard, check out [Get help from a Microsoft reseller or partner](reseller-partner-support.md).</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="6091e-123">要查看包含和不包含通话套餐的商务语音中的内容，请参阅 [Microsoft 365 商务语音服务说明](https://docs.microsoft.com/office365/servicedescriptions/microsoft-365-business-voice-service-description)。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="6091e-123">To see what's included in Business Voice with Calling Plan and without Calling Plan, see [Microsoft 365 Business Voice Service Description](https://docs.microsoft.com/office365/servicedescriptions/microsoft-365-business-voice-service-description).</span></span>
