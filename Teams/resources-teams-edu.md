- 访问 [Teams 教育版](https://www.microsoft.com/en-us/education/products/teams/default.aspx) 页面，查看培训、视频和交互式演示

- 下载 Teams 客户端：[在所有设备上获取 Teams](https://teams.microsoft.com/downloads)
