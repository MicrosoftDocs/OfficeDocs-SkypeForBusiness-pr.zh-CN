## <a name="establish-a-steering-committee"></a><span data-ttu-id="51c93-444">建立指导委员会</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="51c93-444">Establish a steering committee</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="51c93-445">指导委员会是由关键利益干系人与项目负责人共同管理小组，这些利益干系人与项目负责人共同指导项目或计划实现定义的业务成果。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="51c93-445">A steering committee is a governing group of key stakeholders and project leaders who have been brought together to guide a project or program toward its defined business outcomes.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="51c93-446">指导委员会不直接负责项目的交付方式，而负责项目向业务交付什么。 </span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="51c93-446">The steering committee isn't directly responsible for *how* the project is delivered, but rather *what* the project delivers to the business.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="51c93-447">每个项目都需要达成一致的愿景和规定。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="51c93-447">Every project requires an agreed-on vision and charter.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="51c93-448">若要实现项目所需的成果，必须明确定义愿景，并且需要监视和维护该愿景。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="51c93-448">To deliver the outcomes you want from the project, the vision must be clearly defined, and it needs to be monitored and maintained.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="51c93-449">这将成为指导委员会的责任：推动决策、提出建议、提供战略监督、作为组织的项目计划的支持者，并在必要时删除阻止者。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="51c93-449">This becomes the responsibility of the steering committee: to drive decisions, advise, provide strategic oversight, to serve as advocates to the organization for the project's initiatives, and—when necessary—remove blockers.</span></span>
