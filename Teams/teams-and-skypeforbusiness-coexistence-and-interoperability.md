## <a name="interoperability-of-teams-and-skype-for-business"></a><span data-ttu-id="bc169-170">团队和 Skype for business 的互操作性</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="bc169-170">Interoperability of Teams and Skype for Business</span></span>

### <a name="native-interop-and-interop-escalation"></a><span data-ttu-id="bc169-172">本机互操作和互操作升级</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="bc169-172">Native interop and interop escalation</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="bc169-173">有两种类型的互操作体验: 本机和互操作提升。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="bc169-173">There are two types of interop experiences: native and interop escalation.</span></span>
