    - <span data-ttu-id="d7216-115">模板简要和详细说明</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="d7216-115">Template short and long descriptions</span></span>
    - <span data-ttu-id="d7216-116">区域设置可见性</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="d7216-116">Locale visibility</span></span>
