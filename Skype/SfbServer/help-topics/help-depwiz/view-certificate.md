<span data-ttu-id="1a365-p102">在“查看证书”页上，会显示一个窗格，通过此窗格可选择本地证书存储中的任何有效证书。可以选择某个证书，然后单击“查看证书详细信息”。该证书将显示在新窗格中。在“证书”窗格中，可以查看常规证书信息。单击“详细信息”选项卡可显示证书的详细信息，如使用者名称和使用者替代名称。“证书路径”将显示证书的“信任链”路径。查看完证书信息后，单击“确定”。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="1a365-p102">On the **View Certificate** page, there is a pane that enables you to select any valid certificate in the local certificate store. You can select a certificate, and then click **View Certificate Details**. The certificate will be displayed in a new pane. In the **Certificate** pane, you can view the General certificate information. Click the **Details** tab to display detailed information about the certificate, such as subject name and subject alternative name. The Certification Path will display the path of the "chain of trust" for the certificate. When you are finished reviewing the certificate information, click **OK**.</span></span>
