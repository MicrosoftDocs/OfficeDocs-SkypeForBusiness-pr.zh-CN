<span data-ttu-id="5a368-p104">如果在一个前端池或 Standard Edition 服务器中启用存档，则应为部署中的所有其他前端池和 Standard Edition 服务器启用存档。这是因为需要存档通信内容的用户可能会受邀参加其他池中承载的组 IM 对话或会议。如果承载对话或会议的池上没有启用存档，则无法存档完整会话。在这些情况下，仍可以为启用了存档的 IM 的用户进行存档，但不能为会议内容文件以及会议加入或离开事件存档。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="5a368-p104">If you enable archiving on one Front End pool or Standard Edition Server, you should enable it for all other Front End pools and Standard Edition Servers in your deployment. If archiving is not enabled on the pool where the conversation or meeting is hosted, the complete session may not be archived. In these cases, IMs with archiving-enabled users still can be archived, but not for conferencing content files, and conference join or leave events.</span></span>
