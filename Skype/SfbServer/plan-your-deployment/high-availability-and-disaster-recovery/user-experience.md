<span data-ttu-id="21c6e-109">池出现故障时用户拥有的任何呼叫、会议或对话将中断，并且用户必须在故障转移后重新建立这些会话以继续。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="21c6e-109">Any calls, meetings, or conversations a user has when the pool fails are disrupted, and the user must re-establish those sessions after failover to continue.</span></span>
