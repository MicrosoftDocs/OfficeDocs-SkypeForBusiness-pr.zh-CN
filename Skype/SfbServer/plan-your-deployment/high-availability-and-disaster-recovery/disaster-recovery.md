## <a name="recovery-time-for-pool-failover-and-pool-failback"></a><span data-ttu-id="8bb35-130">池故障转移和池故障回复的恢复时间</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="8bb35-130">Recovery time for pool failover and pool failback</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="8bb35-131">对于池故障转移和池故障回复，恢复时间目标 (RTO) 的工程目标为 15-20 分钟。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="8bb35-131">For pool failover and pool failback, the engineering target for recovery time objective (RTO) is 15-20 minutes.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="8bb35-132">这是管理员确定存在灾难并启动故障转移过程之后，故障转移发生所需的时间。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="8bb35-132">This is the time required for the failover to happen, after administrators have determined there was a disaster and started the failover procedures.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="8bb35-133">此时间不包括管理员评估情况并作出决策所需的时间，也不包括用户在故障转移完成后再次登录所需的时间。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="8bb35-133">It does not include the time for administrators to assess the situation and make a decision, nor does it include the time for users to sign in again after failover is complete.</span></span>

## <a name="front-end-pool-pairing-data-security"></a><span data-ttu-id="8bb35-152">前端池配对数据安全</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="8bb35-152">Front End pool pairing data security</span></span>
